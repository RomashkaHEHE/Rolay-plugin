# Searchable Room Activity Or Errors

Status: CANDIDATE
Priority: Low
Last reviewed: 2026-04-22

## Idea

Provide searchable in-plugin access to room activity or error history.

## Why It Matters

- Could reduce back-and-forth when debugging rooms with many events.

## User Feedback

- User does not see strong personal value, but is fine with it if it helps the developer.

## Risks / Constraints

- Easy to overbuild; may be better as a developer-only surface.

## Good Entry Points

- logging strategy
- settings/debug UI
